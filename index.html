About CADers

CADers — short for Computer Aided Designers — is the premier CAD club at Khulna University of Engineering & Technology (KUET). Our mission is to "promote engineering language among engineering students and produce quality designers."

We organize hands-on workshops on industry-standard software like SolidWorks, AutoCAD, and ABAQUS, bridging the gap between classroom theory and real-world design. From 3D modeling to FEA simulation, our members explore the full spectrum of computer-aided engineering.
